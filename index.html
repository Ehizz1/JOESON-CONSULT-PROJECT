About Us

Joeson Consult Limited is a professional consulting firm committed to delivering high-quality services in data protection compliance, data auditing, capacity development,IT services and business advisory. As a licensed Data Protection Compliance Organisation (DPCO), the company plays a key role in helping organizations achieve regulatory compliance while strengthening their information management and governance systems.

Our approach combines expertise, innovation, and practical solutions to help clients navigate complex regulatory landscapes, implement robust IT systems, manage supply chains, and execute projects efficiently—building sustainable systems of excellence along the way.
